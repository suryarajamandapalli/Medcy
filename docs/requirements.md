# Medcy Landing Page: Requirements Document

## 1. Project Overview
Medcy is seeking to establish its technical identity as a provider of **AI-Native Digital Front-Office (DFO) Infrastructure**. The primary objective is to build a high-trust, B2B clinical infrastructure landing page that targets clinic owners, medical partners, and investors.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Clinic Owners**: Looking for scalable infrastructure to unify sales and operations.
- **Medical Partners**: Interested in deterministic, compliant virtual clinic deployments.
- **Investors**: Seeking to understand the edge-tokenized data engine and borderless growth potential.

## 3. Functional Requirements

### 3.1. Infrastructure Narrative Flow
- **Hero Section**: Must define the "AI-Native Engine" narrative with deterministic metrics (Active Portfolios, Edge Tokenization, Borderless Scale).
- **Problem/Solution Grid**: Side-by-side comparative analysis highlighting legacy bottlenecks vs. Medcy's isolated DFO layer.
- **Dynamic Indicators**: High-impact pulsing alerts (Red for problems, Green for solutions) to provide real-time visual feedback.

### 3.2. Portfolio Showcase
- **Module Presentation**: Detailed showcase of specialized clinical OS modules (Janmasethu, Akshayasethu, Kalpasethu).
- **Interactive Details**: 3D flip card mechanism for deep-dives into "Strategy Focus" and module-specific specialties.

### 3.3. Technology Engine
- **Feature Grid**: Showcase of borderless virtual clinics, deterministic logic, and AI orchestration.
- **Interactive Engagement**: Hover-responsive cards with glassmorphic styling and spring-loaded animations.

### 3.4. Lead Capture
- **Partner Inquiries**: Dual-modal lead capture system triggered by "Partner With Us" and "Request Partnership Deck" CTAs.
- **Data Persistence**: Successful integration with Supabase for secure data handling.

### 3.5. Architectural Backgrounds
- **Premium Visualization**: Use of WebGL-based shader animations (SoftAurora) to create a sense of depth and operational "heartbeat" without over-cluttering the UI.
- **Interactive Layers**: Background elements must respond to user input (mouse interaction) to reinforce the AI-native, reactive engine narrative.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements

### 4.1. Design Aesthetics (Enterprise Cobalt)
- **Visual Identity**: Must adhere to the "Enterprise Cobalt" theme — Deep Space Navy background with Electric Blue and Cyan accents.
- **Typography**: Precision pairing of **PT Serif** (Architectural Headlines) and **Abhaya Libre** (Authoritative Subtext).
- **Edge-to-Edge Layout**: Full-width architectural balance in the navigation and hero sections.

### 4.2. Performance & Delivery
- **Single Page Narrative**: Strategic use of vertical space to ensure primary value propositions fit within single viewports.
- **Zero-Delta Brand Integrity**: Preservation of the core Medcy logo and clinical identity throughout technical refactors.

### 4.3. Technical Standards
- **Framework**: Next.js 16 (App Router) for SEO-optimized server-side rendering.
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first fluid layout with vertical stacking for complex grids.
- **Compliance**: Infrastructure-first messaging regarding HIPAA, DPDP, and zero data residency risk.
